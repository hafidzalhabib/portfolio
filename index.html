About Me

I am a fresh graduate in Statistics from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) Surabaya, with a concentration in Computational Statistics and Data Science. I have hands-on experience in various aspects of data analysis, including data exploration, statistical modeling, and data visualization. I am proficient in several tools and programming languages such as Python, R, SQL, Minitab, SPSS, and Excel.

During my academic journey, I was involved in several data-related projects, including developing dashboards, conducting data cleaning and preprocessing, and performing statistical analysis to support decision-making. I am a fast learner, a dedicated team player, and highly adaptable to new environments and challenges.

My strong foundation in statistical thinking, combined with technical skills and a passion for continuous learning, allows me to contribute effectively to dynamic and data-driven environments.
